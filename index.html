Who Am I?

To keep it short and simple, I'm a 28 year old Air Force veteran who fell in love with coding while in the service. I was in for 6 years, and during my enlistment I started coding during my free time. I quickly found that I greatly enjoyed it, and rather than re-enlisting I decided to get out and pursue a career in Web Development. I'm currently pursuing my CS degree, which I temporarily put on hold in order to complete Code Platoon. I spend basically all of my free time coding, learning as much as I possibly can while improving my skills, but when I'm not busy coding I do enjoy gaming and watching sci-fi/horror flicks.
